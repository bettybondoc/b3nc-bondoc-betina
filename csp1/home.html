We know how wedding preps can give even the most organized people we know a panic attack so we want to simplify everything for you. Planning your wedding may look scary and overwhelming because of the amount of decisions to be made but trust us when we say you can get through this.

On this website, we've listed down the basic requirements to achieve your dream wedding. Below, you'll find a 6-month wedding timeline to help you get started. Of course, the longer the planning time, the better. But who says you can't get married in 6 months, right? So, inhale, exhale and enjoy the ride.

Wedding Timeline

  • 6 months before

    Envision what you want (decide on a theme!) and draw up a budget

    Research on wedding requirements and suppliers

    Book your wedding hall / church and reception venue (this will set your wedding date in stone)

    Book your caterer (after a bunch of food-tasting, of course)

    Book your couturier

  • 5 months before

    Make a shortlist of your entourage and guest list

    Book your wedding photographer and videographer

    Order your save-the-date and/or invites

    Book your event stylist / florist (or start planning if you plan to DIY)

    Send out Save-the-Date cards or e-vites

  • 4 months before

    Start gathering requirements (government / church)

    Book your Hair and Make-up Artist

  • 3 months before

    Prenup shoot (hit two birds with one stone by asking your HMUA to do the test make-up on this day)

    Apply for marriage license

    Order wedding favors

  • 2 months before

    Send out wedding invites

    Send complete requirements to church

    Check with your stylist regarding the event set-up

    Book a same-day coordinator (if you're didn't hire a full-wedding coordinator)

    Start writing your wedding vows

  • 1 month before

    Finalize your guest list (RSVP)

    Go for your final fitting

    Prepare emergency wedding kits

    Finalize the program

    Turnover everything to your coordinator / bridesmaids
